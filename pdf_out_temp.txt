1)Title: 13 poker players arrested
Source: Sakshi
Date: 14/06/2025
Nellimarla Rural: Kondavela in the mandalPolice conducted raids on a poker establishment
in Gada village on Friday. Acting on prior information, the personnel under the command
of Sub-Inspector Ganesh conducted raids and arrested 13 people who were playing poker
near the local Seetha Lake. The Sub-Inspector said that Rs. 8,860 in cash was seized from
them. He asked the people to inform him if anyone is involved in poker, cockfighting and
other anti-social activities in the villages.
2)Title: Auto Gang Khel Khatam
Source: Sakshi
Date: 14/06/2025
Srikakulam: The Second Town Police of Srikakulam district have successfully busted an
auto theft gang involved in a case of stealing from an elderly couple. On June 5, Korada
Ramana and his wife Ramalakshmi, residents of Sakivanipet, Kothapet Panchayat,
Etcherla Mandal, were robbed while traveling in an auto. The couple immediately filed a
complaint at the Second Town Police Station, prompting Sub-Inspector Rama Rao to
initiate an investigation.
During vehicle inspections at Thotapalem Junction around 10 a.m. on Friday, SI Rama Rao
and his team stopped a Bajaj Maxima auto (AP 30VD440) after suspecting suspicious
activity. Upon detaining the passengers and conducting a detailed inquiry at the station, the
police identified the suspects as:
● Gorre Mounika – Resident of Pettada village, Mentada mandal, Vizianagaram
district
● Gorre Peddintlamma – Mounika’s mother
● Mekala Hari – Auto driver and nephew of Peddintlamma, from Cheedivalasa,
Kothavalasa mandal
All three hail from Vizianagaram district and are confirmed to be part of an organized theft
gang. SI Rama Rao revealed that the trio have over ten criminal cases registered against
them in various districts, including rural regions in Srikakulam. The gang appears to have
a broader network, and further investigation is underway to identify more accomplices.
