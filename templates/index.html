Intelligence Capabilities

📡

Automated Ingestion

Continuously collects crime news from GNews and NewsAPI across configured districts. Covers print, e-paper, and digital sources.

🔍

Relevance Filtering

AI-powered classification identifies crime-relevant articles using configurable include/exclude keywords and crime categories.

🗂️

Story Clustering

Intelligently groups articles reporting the same incident across multiple publishers using TF-IDF similarity analysis.

⚖️

Competing Views

Identifies factual discrepancies and tone differences when multiple sources report the same event differently.

📋

Daily Digest

Generates structured, district-wise daily intelligence digests from online news or uploaded offline documents (PDF/DOCX).

📄

PDF Reports

Exports clustered intelligence reports with summaries, source comparisons, and metadata in professional PDF format.

Use Case Scenarios

Article Ingestion

Collect and organize news articles from various newspapers — text-based and image-based content.

Relevance Selection

Automatically classify articles as relevant or irrelevant based on district-specific criteria and crime keywords.

Article Grouping

Group articles discussing the same incident or topic across different sources with assigned cluster IDs.

Competing Views

Identify articles that report the same event with varying facts, perspectives, or tone across sources.

Summary Generation

Generate concise, AI-powered summaries for each grouped topic for rapid situation awareness.

Daily Digest

Generate the final daily digest per district with topic, source, and summarized article content.
